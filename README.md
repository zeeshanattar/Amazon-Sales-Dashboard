# Amazon-Sales-Dashboard
# 1)Project Title
Amazon Sales Dashboard to give sellers a clear, real-time view of business performance so they can make faster, data-driven decisions.

# 2) Purpose
dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, helping sellers increase revenue, improve profitability, and scale efficiently.

# 3) Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

Power BI Desktop: Main data visualization platform used for report creation.

Power Query: Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.

Data Modeling: Relationships established among tables to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.

File Format: .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews

# 4) Data Source
This dashboard is built using sample/synthetic e-commerce data modeled to represent Amazon-like operations.

# 5) Features and Highlights
# 5.1) Bussiness Problem
This Amazon sales dashboard is to solve business problem of data fragmentation and lack of actionable insight, enabling sellers to control performance, reduce losses, and grow profitably.

# 5.2) Goal Of The DashBoard
The goal of this Amazon sales dashboard is to turn Amazon sales data into clear insights that drive smarter decisions, higher sales, and sustainable business growth.

# 5.3) Walk Through The Key Visuals
The purpose of creating a Total Customers KPI is to measure the size and growth of a businessâ€™s customer base, which is essential for evaluating market reach and long-term sustainability.

The purpose of a Sales KPI is to quantify sales success and guide actions that increase revenue and business growth.

The purpose of creating a Profit KPI is to measure how much value the business actually earns after costs, ensuring that growth is sustainable and financially healthy.

The purpose of creating an Orders KPI is to measure sales activity and demand volume, helping businesses understand how often customers are placing purchases and how efficiently orders are being processed.

The purpose of making a Top 5 Profitable Sub-Categories clustered bar chart is to visually compare and highlight the sub-categories that contribute the most profit, enabling quick and informed business decisions.

The purpose of making a Sales by Category clustered bar chart is to compare sales performance across different product categories clearly and effectively, helping stakeholders identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities.

The purpose of a Monthly Sales per Year line chart is to visualize sales performance over time, identify seasonal patterns, and compare year-over-year growth to support better business decisions.

The purpose of making an Average Profit and Sales per Quarter clustered column chart is to compare profitability and sales performance across quarters in a clear, visual way, helping businesses track performance, spot trends, and make strategic decisions.

The purpose of a Region Slicer is to enable dynamic filtering and regional analysis, helping businesses make geographically informed decisions efficiently.

The purpose of an Orders by Payment Mode pie chart is to visually represent the distribution of orders by payment type, helping businesses understand customer preferences and optimize payment operations.

The purpose of a Return Status pie chart is to show the distribution of order returns and completion status, helping businesses track returns and improve customer satisfaction and operations.

The purpose of making a Geo-Distribution Map is to visually display where your customers, sales, or other business activities are located geographically, helping businesses make region-specific decisions.

The purpose of making a Profit by Category pie chart is to visually show how total profit is distributed across different product categories, helping businesses identify which categories contribute most to profitability.

The purpose of a Cities by Sales treemap is to visually compare sales across cities, making it easier to identify high-value markets and opportunities for growth.

# 5.4) Bussiness Impact And Insight
This Dashboards make business monitoring faster, more accurate, and actionable.They reveal trends, opportunities, and problems that help optimize sales, profit, and operations.
